Frequently Asked Questions

  • What are the qualifications of your doctors?

    Our doctors have extensive training and experience in their respective specialties, with many holding board certifications and advanced degrees.

  • How do I make an appointment with a specific doctor?

    You can request an appointment with a specific doctor through our website or by calling our receptionist. We will do our best to accommodate your request.

  • What if I need to see a doctor urgently?

    Our hospital offers emergency care services 24/7. If you need to see a doctor urgently, please visit our emergency department or call 911.

  • Do you accept insurance?

    Yes, we accept a wide range of insurance plans. Please check with your insurance provider to confirm coverage and eligibility.

  • Can I see a doctor for a second opinion?

    Yes, we welcome second opinion consultations. Please speak with your primary care physician or call us to schedule an appointment with one of our specialists.
